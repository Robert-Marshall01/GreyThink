Code of Conduct
===============

This project follows the Contributor Covenant code of conduct. By
participating, you agree to abide by its terms.

Standards
---------

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing other peopleâ€™s private information (doxing)
- Any conduct that is abusive, harassing, discriminatory, or otherwise
  unprofessional

Enforcement
-----------

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported by contacting the project maintainers via an issue or email. Reports
will be handled and investigated promptly and fairly.

Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include temporary or permanent
ban from the project, removal of privileges, or notifying relevant
authorities when appropriate.

Contact
-------

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or
contact the maintainers. Replace this line with a direct contact email if you
prefer a private report.
